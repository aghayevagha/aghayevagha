About Me

I am a research assistant at Azerbaijan Technical University under the supervision of Dr. Yadigar Imamverdiyev, and adjunct instructor at BHOS, Baku, Azerbaijan. Aside from that, I also have experience as a machine learning engineer in industry, with a closer focus on satellite imagery.
My primary research interests include cryptography: with a particular interest in lattice-based cryptography and homomorphic encryption for privacy-preserving technologies.
